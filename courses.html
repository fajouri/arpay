Automation and Compliance in the Digital Age

Syllabus Overview

This course equips participants with practical skills in automation, compliance, and ethical AI development. Explore key topics, regulations, and hands-on projects designed for IT professionals and legal experts.

Course Modules

1. Introduction to Regulatory Compliance

Understand the importance of compliance in digital ecosystems with a focus on GDPR, E-Privacy Directive, and the EU AI Act.

Practical Exercise: Create a flowchart to identify compliance requirements for a given process.

Duration: 2 hours

2. Fundamentals of Automation in Compliance

Learn about automation tools and build bots for compliance processes using UiPath or Automation Anywhere.

Practical Exercise: Develop a bot to process consent forms and log them in a database.

Duration: 3 hours
